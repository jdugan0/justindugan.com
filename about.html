About Me

I am someone who loves to make things to learn more and teach those experiences to others. I am driven by a desire to work with people to help them grow.

Background

I'm from Glenmoore, Pennsylvania, and went to a small high school called the University Scholars Program. I had great relationships with my teachers there that encouraged me to dive deep into whatever caught my attention. Questions about complex numbers led to my fractal renderer. Questions about how RAM worked led to my 6502 computer. At Georgia Tech, I am trying to explore my passions discover where I can do the most enjoyable and impactful work.
